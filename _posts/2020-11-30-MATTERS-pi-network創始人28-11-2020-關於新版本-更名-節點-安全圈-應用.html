‎關於節點：‎

@Sharat The less times a node changes IP the better. Even more important is your ports to be accessible by other nodes. Otherwise other nodes can’t find you. 

‎節點改變IP的次數越少越好。 更重要的是，其他節點可以訪問您的埠。 否則，其他節點將無法找到您。‎

@azroi Phase 3 will be after we have developed a robust network of nodes. The project is evolving very well. But it is not going to be ready next week 

 We will have more nodes following the blockchain very soon though, in the coming week or two.

@suppersai A changing IP is a negative factor for supernodes. That’s because other nodes need to know your IP to connect to you. 

‎IP的變化對超級節點來說是一個負面因素。 這是因為其他節點需要知道您的IP才能連接到您。‎

‎關於安全圈‎

@pi255666 My experience is that most people have others they actually know in their security circle. Only a minority of people have too many unknown people in their security circles and earning teams. Information about Pi travels word of mouth among friends. That’s why it’s invite only. There are also some influencers and people who post at random places and gather random people in their earning teams but I don’t think they constitute the majority. I think our security circles are pretty good and people have the opportunity to update them every day. In the future when the blockchain is there I think people will be even more careful about their security circles especially after one can recover their private keys though their security circle. (You would never trust your ability to recover your lost wallet to strangers) 

‎@pi255666我的經驗是，大多數人在他們的安全圈子裡都有真正認識的人。 只有少數人在他們的安全圈和賺錢團隊中有太多的陌生人。 關於圓周率的消息在朋友之間傳播。 這就是為什麼它只邀請。 也有一些有影響力的人，他們隨便在什麼地方發佈資訊，在他們的賺錢團隊中隨機召集一些人，但我不認為他們是大多數。 我認為我們的安全圈相當不錯，人們有機會每天更新它們。 在未來，當區塊鏈在那裡，我認為人們會更加小心他們的安全圈，特別是在一個人可以恢復他們的私人密鑰通過他們的安全圈。 （你永遠不會相信自己有能力為陌生人找回丟失的錢包）‎
