【By: To To】

My favourite festival is Durian Day. It is because this is a festival that I created by myself.

Durian Day doesn’t have a long history. It is just a day created for durian lovers to eat and enjoy durian all day long.

Great. Now you know all the facts, write it down, memorize it, and tell all your families and friends.

And guess what? Today is the durian day! My grandfather had been growing a mega durian for the whole year. Now it is as big as a truck wheel.  And now it is time for a happy durian feat.
