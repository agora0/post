I can tell my mom doesn't really want to stay at home when she knows the pandemic in Hong Kong is stabilized now, she asked "hey son, do you have interested to go eat Dim Sum outside today?"

Of course I know her meaning is "Hey son, let's accompany your mom to have Dim Sum outside", I therefore pretend I am too excited to know that we are going to eat Dim Sum outside, well, I am not cheating, I just act a bit extravagant and it helps to make my mom happier, no harm done, right?

The weather is really hotter and hotter in Hong Kong now, I was sweating once I go down to the street, fortunately we didn't have to wait too long for the bus, we immediately get into the shopping mall once when got off from the bus. The Dim Sum restaurant is inside the mall, so we can enjoy the super strong air-con there, cool

We were a bit late for the afternoon tea period so we didn't have to queue anymore as everyone already get inside and have table. Good to both my mom and me, I hate standing there to wait !!!

Now the Chinese Dim Sum restaurant is getting more modern now, they no longer using the paper order form, they ask people to scan the QR code to place order via wechat now, they only give the paper order form to the elder people now, it's good to see such change, we can save more paper to help on the environmental protection.

The bill wasn't too expensive for today's afternoon tea, it was cheaper that the restaurant in Kung Tong but the foods quality are not as good as the Kung Tong one anyway.
