We have entered a new era in which blockchain and decentralization are changing our inherent online lifestyles. But as fascinating as the concept of decentralization is, Web3 is still far inferior to the basic services of the Web2 era in terms of user experience.The birth of the DAS domain name has improved this problem to a certain extent, providing a DNS-like service for the crypto world. After using the DAS domain name, users can resolve the custom sugud.bit to any public link address such as ETH/BSC/Polygon/TRON.

Transferring money to a .bit address in a transfer is equivalent to transferring money to a complex public chain address, improving the security of wallet transfers. On platforms or tools that support DAS, such as on-chain browsers, the user address will also be displayed directly as a .bit address.

DAS is a decentralized account system based on Nervos CKB. Different from the centralized account system such as mobile phone number/mailbox/social account, DAS is a blockchain-based, decentralized open source account system. It is currently the first account system with broad compatibility and full ownership and control by the user.There are many DAS users, DAS not only as a digital currency collection account, but also as an account to access general Internet services, and even as a domain name!

DAS also has the following powerful and convenient functions: as a personal information homepage, as a passwordless login credential, as an end-to-end encrypted communication account, a symbol of honor! It's a passport to the web3 era!DAS is committed to the development of a cross-chain decentralized account system in the web3 era, making great contributions to the construction of DID infrastructure in the new era!

New feature: Reverse Record

When you set the reverse record, the relevant information needs to be stored on the blockchain, and it requires 201 CKB of storage space. So please make sure your balance is greater than 201 CKB before setting a reverse record.Currently, DAS DApp and BestDAS support DAS reverse Record, Apps integration and more applications on the road.

Start

Log in DAS Registrar and click on “Reverse Record”, and then set a DAS account.The reverse record can be set to any DAS account. To ensure the accuracy of the reverse record, any of the following conditions must be met for the reverse record to be valid.

* Your address is the Owner / Manager of the DAS account.

* Your address is in the records of the DAS account.

For example, set the reverse record of the ETH address 0x4f67…1279 to sugus.bit. This will take effect as long as any of the following conditions is met.

* The ETH address 0x4f67…1279 is the Owner / Manager of the DAS account.

* The ETH address 0x4f67…1279 is in the record of the DAS account.
