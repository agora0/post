──性別。社會──

1.【性別化創新：連結知識研發與社會改革的五道工法】（台大圖書館Master講堂）

活動網址：reurl.cc/an51KX

時間：4月26日（二）14:30-16:00
主旨：「性別化創新」（gendered innovation）是21世紀國際科學社群與科技政策倚重的取向。國際頂尖的科技研究持續檢討研究的性別盲點，包括老鼠研究取樣偏重公鼠，AI研究複製性別偏見，都是經典的案例。而「性別化創新」不只要避免性別盲，更強調善用性別分析工具，運用在科學、健康醫療、工程與環境等等研究，以能創造出更精良的創新研究。本次演講將簡介性別化創新的具體操作方法，並呈現國際與本土透過此方法而達到社會改革的案例。
主講：吳嘉苓（台大社會系教授）

2.【It looked so attractive, and I couldn’t resist”: Porn-Watching Experiences Amongst Chinese Youth】（Manchester China Institute）

活動網址：reurl.cc/o19753

時間：4月28日（四）19:30-21:00 台北時間
主旨：Porn has been identified as one of the most significant factors affecting sexuality-related experiences in the everyday lives of the Chinese youth. In particular, it has been recognised as a “cultural product” in China. In this talk, the speaker will first introduce why it is necessary to investigate the youth experiences of porn-watching within China’s societal context, and how differentiated aspirations shape their porn-watching experiences. Subsequently, the talk will examine the phenomenon of the Chinese youth calling porn stars “teachers” and its relation to the absence of adequate sexuality education in China. The seminar will conclude by discussing the dilemma that the Chinese youth faces today between increasing economic freedom on the one hand and tightening political and cultural ideologies on the other.
主講：Chong Liu（PhD candidate, University of Leeds）
