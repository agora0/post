The waves shoving ship on the sea
Pianist's fingers touching melody
Playing like a love messenger
Warming the shaken mind strings
Winter has a music box
Collects sounds of the dreamers' faiths
As the spring rain falling again
It will become a great star
Winter has a music box
The moon has a tears case
Winter has a music box
Deep sea has a dream case
The winds pushing ship from the sky
Singer's lips blowing melody
Singing like a love messenger
Warming the cold mind strings
Winter has a music box
Collects sounds of the lovers' feels
As the spring rain falling again
It will become a shining sun
Winter has a music box
The moon has a lighting case
Winter has a music box
Great sky has a dream case
