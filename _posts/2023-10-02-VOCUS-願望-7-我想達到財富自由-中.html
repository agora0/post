大家應該都知道歐普拉（Oprah Gail Winfrey）吧？她是美國最具影響力的非洲裔名人之一兼《時代百大人物 / Time 100》。

她在某次訪問中說到：

People get to where they want to go because they know where they want to go … The reason that some people live in chaos is because there’s chaos in their minds. As soon as you get clear, it cleans up.
