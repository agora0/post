How do Hong Kong people live during 2022 Chief Executive Election？

Hong Kong’s leadership election was held on 8 May 2022 in Wan Chai for the new term of the Chief Executive. John Lee Ka-chiu, the sole candidate, has been confirmed to be the city’s Chief Executive-elect. A new page has just been written for Hong Kong’s history. While in the streets, apparently unconcerned by the election, Hong Kong citizens live as usual. This daily phenomenon seems to become unusual.
