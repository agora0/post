保護傘 Aegis

⭐️⭐️⭐️

🇹🇼台灣。台北市。公館

是律師黃國桐為了協助在台尋求庇護的香港人🇭🇰而開的餐廳，店內裝潢和擺設「反送中」相關的作品和書籍，以此記錄這段歷史。

提供義大利麵、焗烤和飲品等，食物味道普通，但份量蠻大份，很適合學生族群😂

基於餐廳的宗旨，想支持的人都可以來吃喔🎗

🇹🇼 Taiwan, Taipei City

The restaurant run by lawyer 黃國桐 to assist Hong Kong asylum seekers🇭🇰 in Taiwan is decorated with works and books on the subject to document this history.

The restaurant provides pasta, drinks, and so on. The food tastes ordinary, but the portions are quite large. It's suitable for students 😂

Based on the tenet of the restaurant, anyone who wants to support you can come🎗
